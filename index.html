FAQ
Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does payment work?
You send a $100 deposit via CashApp ($kinsleybazemore8) or Venmo (@Mrbazemore) to get started. The remaining balance is due when your site is done and you approve it — before we hand over the files. Simple as that.
How fast can you build my site?
Most sites are done in 3–5 business days after we receive your deposit and your business info. Elite packages get priority treatment and can be done in as little as 72 hours.
Do I need to know anything about websites?
Not at all. Just tell us your business name, what you do, and what you want it to look like. We handle all the design, writing, and technical setup. You just approve and launch.
Will I actually own my website?
100%. Once you pay in full, everything is transferred to you — the files, the domain, the hosting credentials, analytics access. No ongoing fees, no subscriptions, no lock-in. It's yours forever.
What if I don't like the design?
We send you a live preview before the site goes live. If you want changes, we make them. We don't launch anything until you love it. Revisions are included in every package.
Can you build an online store?
Yes — the Elite package includes a full e-commerce store where you can sell products, manage inventory, and accept payments. Perfect for clothing brands, retail, and product businesses.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Absolutely. We're 100% remote and work with clients worldwide. Everything is handled online — no location restrictions.
Can you help me get found on Google?
Yes. Every site we build is SEO-ready. The Pro and Elite packages include full SEO setup, Google Business profile, Google Analytics, and Search Console to help you rank faster and track your traffic.
