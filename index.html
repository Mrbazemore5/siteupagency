FAQ
Common questions.

Still curious? Email kinsleybazemore@gmail.com and we'll get right back to you.

01 What types of content can SiteUp actually create? +
SiteUp generates documents (Word, PDF, PowerPoint, Excel), media (music, videos, images, voice), code (Python, JavaScript), research reports with web data, mathematical analyses, spreadsheets with formulas, and much more. If you can describe it, SiteUp can create it.
02 How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude? +
ChatGPT and Claude give you text. SiteUp gives you actual files — a PowerPoint you can open, an MP3 you can play, a video you can post. We use specialized AI agents for each content type, not a single chat model trying to do everything.
03 Can it do research and use real data from the web? +
Yes. SiteUp agents search the web in real time, extract data from live sources, and incorporate accurate, cited information into your content — not just training data from a cutoff date.
04 What about images, music, and videos? +
SiteUp uses state-of-the-art AI models for each media type — image generation for visuals, music synthesis for audio, and video generation for motion content. All output is download-ready in standard formats.
05 Is the content professionally formatted and usable? +
Yes. Documents open in Word or Google Docs with proper headings, tables, and citations. Spreadsheets have working formulas. Presentations have designed slides. Videos are properly encoded MP4 files.
06 Can I make changes after SiteUp creates something? +
Absolutely. Just describe what you want changed — "make it shorter," "add a section on competitors," "use a darker color scheme." SiteUp will regenerate or refine exactly what you ask.
07 How long does it take to create content? +
Most content takes under 60 seconds. Complex research reports or multi-scene videos may take 2–3 minutes. You'll see a live progress indicator throughout.
08 Who owns the content SiteUp creates? +
You do. 100%. Everything SiteUp generates belongs to you — no licensing fees, no royalty claims, no restrictions on commercial use.
09 What AI models do you use? +
We use a mix of frontier models for each task — leading LLMs for text and reasoning, dedicated models for image generation, synthesis engines for music, and video generation models for motion content. Always the best tool for each job.
