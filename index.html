FAQ
Everything you need to know about how we work.
What exactly is the free preview? +
We build a real, fully functional prototype of your website — with your business name, services, and area. It's not a mockup image or a Figma file — it's a live, clickable website. We show you a video walkthrough and send you the link. You pay nothing until you decide you want to move forward with the full build.
What is the $69 kickoff fee for? +
The $69 kickoff fee is paid only after you approve the preview. It kicks off the full build — where we take your real logo, photos, copy, and content and build the final version of your site. Nothing starts until you've said yes.
Do I own my website? Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — 100%. Once you pay the final balance at launch, we hand you everything: your domain login, hosting access, and all the files. It's completely yours. No lock-in, no monthly obligation unless you want ongoing management.
How do revisions work? +
Unlimited revisions until you're satisfied — before you pay the final balance. After launch, just text us any changes and they're done same day. No tickets, no portals.
Do I have to get on a call? +
Nope. The entire process can happen over text. We prefer it that way — fast, no scheduling, no hold music. You fill out the form, we text you, and we get to work.
How is this different from building it myself on Wix or Squarespace? +
On Wix or Squarespace, you're on your own. You spend hours figuring it out, it still looks like a template, and you still have to manage it forever. We build it for you, make it look professional, and you text us when something needs changing. Your time is worth more than a DIY website project.
What do you need from me before you start? +
For the free preview — nothing. Just fill out the form. For the full build after you approve, we'll need your logo (if you have one), any photos of your work, your list of services, and any copy/text you want on the site. We can also write the copy for you if needed.
